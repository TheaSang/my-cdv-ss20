What is fandom? To many fans, “Fandom is a way of life”. Fandom is a group of people that collaboratively creates an archive around a source text. Fandom is a gathering around “common media” and a common love. Fandom is production and creation. Fandom can and will form around any cultural media product that sparks interest in its audience. The most popular types of fandom tend to form around TV shows and movies, as well as books and video games, with different fandoms then forming different communities underneath the unifying umbrella of love for the source text.

For many fans, there usually belongs to different fandoms and may have strong desire as well as great creativity onloadedmetadata to construct an imaginative world for characters in different source text encounter with eath other. Thus, it is common to see one piece of fanfictions cross different fandoms. The cross-fandoms fanfictions demonstate the infinite possibility of fans' imgaination.
